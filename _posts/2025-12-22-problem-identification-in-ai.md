A **Guide to Problem Identification in Artificial Intelligence** helps you map your input data to the right AI approach.

### Input Data Types and Corresponding AI Sub‑fields

1. **Number** – *Machine Learning (ML)*
   - Regression, classification, clustering.
2. **Image or Number** – *Deep Learning (DL)*
   - Computer vision, object detection, facial recognition.
3. **Text** – *Natural Language Processing (NLP)*
   - Sentiment analysis, translation, chatbots.
4. **Date/Time** – *Time‑Series Analysis*
   - Forecasting, anomaly detection, seasonal trends.

#### Why It Matters
Understanding your data type lets you quickly choose the most suitable AI technique, saving time and improving results.
